Send Us Feedback

We'd love to hear from you. Tell us what's working, what's not, or share how one of our published activities went in your classroom. Every response helps us improve the site and gives other educators useful real-classroom insight.

What Kind of Feedback Are We Looking For?

Activity Feedback You tried one of our published activities — tell us how it went, what worked, what you'd change
General Feedback Anything about the site itself: what's helpful, what's confusing, what's missing
Suggestions & Ideas Resources we should add, frameworks to consider, features that would help you
Bugs or Problems Something broken, confusing navigation, or a link that doesn't work

How We Use Feedback

Substantive feedback about specific activities — especially from people who've taught them — may be added to the activity's published page (with your permission and credit) so other educators can benefit from real classroom experience. General feedback shapes how we develop the site over time.

We read every response. We may not be able to reply to every one, but we genuinely consider all feedback. If you'd like a personal response, leave your email and let us know.
